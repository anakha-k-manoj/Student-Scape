All the teachers supports and care for us a lot. We have a lot of activities to entertain us thoughout the year. Such as:

1.Electives-

 Every year, students get to select an activity they want to try or pursue for the coming year. The selection is on first come for serve basis. Some activities are paid, like skating, robotics,lawn tennis and mathwizz.There are a few language based such as i-Speak French, i-Speak German and i-Speak Arbic. Then there are a few related to arts such as, Dance and Choreography, Props and Artistic Creations and Music Club. Then the sports onesare Football and swimming. We have self defense. There are ones that help you with public speaking, those are Global Voices and Young Economist. Then there are a few extra activities, that include coding, critical thinking,etc. which are 3D Printing, Chess, Code Warriors and Design Thinking.

2.ADM-

ADM is Art, Dance and Music. Each term according to alphabetical order, roll number wise, a specific number of students go for each of the period assigned for them. During term 1, the first 10 kids in the class list go for art, the next 10 go for music and the last 10 go for dance. It shuffles every term. Every class we get an assignment to complete by next class.

3.Inter-house Competitions-

At the starting of term 1, a portal opens, which is also on first come first serve basis, in which we have to choose a sport and an activity of our choice. Through the year, you will be called for an audition or trials for your selected sport(once). If you pass the audition, you move on to the final stage, where you compete against other houses with your house members. If its an audition for cultural activities, you do the performance by yourself and if you place first your house gets more points, but if its sports finals then you will be playing with ur team against another house.

4.Inter School Competitions-

The co-scholastic program of the school enables students to hone their talents and provides a myriad of opportunities for them to participate in local and international school competitions school thus giving them the opportunity to compete with the best in the region and beyond. Event participation details will be sent to you in your CLP inbox.


5.Scholar Badge-

A grading process on the basis of marks scored is often done to facilitate college admission procedures.The school has a scholar badge ladder which awards students for consistent academic endeavour for seven consecutive years from grade 5 to grade 11 culminating with the winning of gold medal by the student. Students are encouraged to work for their scholar badge as it is a very prestigious award bestowed by the school for good consistent academic work.

Now lets talk about our class:

We have CTP period everyday. It's Class Teachers Interactive period. During this period we interact and disscuss about upcoming events, exams, problems faced in class,etc. If you are a new addmission you will be assigned with a buddy or mentor to help you out throughout the term/year. This buddy/mentor is supposed to tell you all about the school, the class and much more.

So you wouldnt have to worry of being left alone. A Whatsapp a group will be made in which all of your classmates and respective teachers will be added. In this group the teachers will inform you about the upcoming important events, timetable, bell timings, teachers name list, necessary items needed, etc. And in Teams also there will be a different team for each subject with the subject teacher. You can call and talk to your friends on this platform.

We have a class known as DC which is held before exams to clear doubts and queries of children (Doubt Clearing Session). Teachers tell a time and a day that everyone is free and hold the meeting for maximum 2 hours.Before the respective subjects exam, for all subjects its held. Not commonly but some times the DC is held during school hours in that specific period.
